Tutor Match

The platform will allow students to browse a diverse range of tutors, each with detailed profiles that highlight their areas of expertise, teaching experience, and ratings from previous students. Students will be able to book one-on-one tutoring sessions directly through the platform, choosing times that best fit their schedules. After each session, students can provide feedback on their experience, which will contribute to the tutor's overall rating. This rating system, along with the tutor"s qualifications and reviews, will enable students to make informed decisions, ensuring they find the best possible match for their educational needs. The goal is to simplify the process of finding quality tutors while fostering an environment of transparency and trust.
